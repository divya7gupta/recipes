🍗 Chicken Biryani

Category: Non-Veg

Difficulty: Hard

Prep Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

An aromatic and flavorful rice dish made with tender chicken, fragrant spices, and basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g Basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 500g Chicken, cut into medium pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 2 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp Biryani masala powder
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • Whole spices: Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom, Bay leaf
  • 4 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large pot, boil water with whole spices and salt. Add the soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Set half aside for garnish.
  4. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook on medium heat until tender and the masala thickens.
  5. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken masala.
  6. Top with fried onions, fresh mint, and coriander leaves.
  7. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat (Dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  8. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salan!

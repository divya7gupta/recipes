🍫 Chocolate Brownies

Category: Dessert

Difficulty: Easy

Prep Time: 35 minutes

Fudgy, rich chocolate brownies that melt in your mouth. Perfect treat!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt the butter and mix it with sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Add vanilla extract and mix.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder.
  6. Slowly fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Don't overmix.
  7. Pour the batter into the greased pan and spread evenly.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes. The center should be slightly gooey.
  9. Let it cool, cut into squares, and dust with powdered sugar. Enjoy!

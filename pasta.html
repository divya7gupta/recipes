🍝 Tomato Basil Pasta

Category: Veg

Difficulty: Easy

Prep Time: 25 minutes

A simple and delicious Italian-style pasta with fresh tomatoes and basil.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or penne pasta
  • 4-5 fresh tomatoes, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and add minced garlic. Sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 8-10 minutes until they break down into a sauce.
  4. Add red chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce and mix until everything is coated evenly.
  6. Turn off the heat, add torn basil leaves, and give a final toss.
  7. Serve hot with grated parmesan on top. Buon appetito!

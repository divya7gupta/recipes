🥞 Fluffy Pancakes

Category: Veg

Difficulty: Easy

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Soft, fluffy pancakes perfect for a lazy weekend breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Maple syrup and berries for topping

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the egg, milk, and melted butter together.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently. Don't overmix — small lumps are okay!
  4. Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat and lightly grease it with butter.
  5. Pour a small ladle of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on top (about 2 minutes).
  6. Flip and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Stack the pancakes, drizzle with maple syrup, and top with fresh berries. Enjoy!

🍗 Butter Chicken

Category: Non-Veg

Difficulty: Medium

Prep Time: 45 minutes

A rich and creamy chicken curry that's a favorite in every Indian household.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  5. Add garam masala and mix well.
  6. Add the cooked chicken and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Pour in the cream, stir gently, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice!

🍚 Vegetable Fried Rice

Category: Veg

Difficulty: Easy

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Quick and tasty fried rice loaded with colorful veggies. Great for lunch!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover, cold rice)
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok on high heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan, scramble them quickly, and push them to one side.
  3. Add the diced carrots, peas, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the cold cooked rice and break up any clumps.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and mix everything together evenly.
  6. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, tossing frequently.
  7. Add sesame oil and chopped green onions. Give a final toss.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious fried rice!
